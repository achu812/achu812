About Suresh Gopi

Suresh Gopi is one of the most renowned actors in the Malayalam film industry. He is known for his powerful performances and his portrayals of tough, no-nonsense characters in films. He has also ventured into politics, serving as a Member of Parliament for the Thrissur constituency in Kerala.

Career in Films

Suresh Gopi made his acting debut in 1986, and soon became one of the most bankable stars in Malayalam cinema. He is known for his iconic roles in films like *"Commissioner" (1994)*, *"Lelam" (1997)*, and *"Rajamanikyam" (2005)*. He earned critical acclaim for his performances in both action and drama genres.

  • Commissioner (1994) - A landmark film in his career, where he portrayed a no-nonsense police officer.
  • Lelam (1997) - A film that solidified his reputation as an action star.
  • Pathram (1999) - A drama that showed his versatility as an actor.
  • Rajamanikyam (2005) - One of his most commercially successful films.

Political Career

In addition to his acting career, Suresh Gopi has been active in politics. He joined the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and became a Member of Parliament in 2016, representing the Thrissur constituency in Kerala. He has worked towards the development of Kerala and has been involved in several social initiatives.

Awards and Recognition

Suresh Gopi has received several awards for his remarkable contributions to Malayalam cinema:

  • Kerala State Film Award - Second Best Actor
  • Filmfare Awards South - Best Actor
  • Asianet Film Awards - Best Actor
